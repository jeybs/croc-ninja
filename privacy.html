Privacy Policy

Croc-Ninja · Last updated: June 2, 2026

Croc-Ninja ("the app") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely offline.

Data We Store

All in-game data — including your high score, settings, and lifetime statistics — is stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted off your device and is never shared with us or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

What the App Does Not Do

Croc-Ninja contains no advertising, no analytics, no third-party tracking SDKs, no user accounts, and no backend servers. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or any other personal information.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no personal data from anyone, it collects no personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
